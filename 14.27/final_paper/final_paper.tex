\section{Dataset Explanation}

The proprietary Panjiva Dataset comes from the back-end databases collected by Panjiva, Inc. Panjiva's website \url{http://www.panjiva.com} acts as a medium for buyers and suppliers of manufactured goods. The site provides a communication platform so that bulk buyers of a particular good can search and obtain unbiased information on factories and suppliers of that good.
